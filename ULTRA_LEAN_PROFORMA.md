### Year 1: Launch to 10,000 Users (~$1M ARR)

**Team: 1-2 people**

| Role | Who | Cost | Notes |
|------|-----|------|-------|
| Founder/CEO | You | $0-100K | Equity-funded or bootstrapped |
| Part-time Support | Contractor | $30K | 20hrs/week offshore |
| **Total** | | **$30-130K** | |

**Why this works:**
- AI (Claude) handles 70%+ of coding, content, copy
- Self-service product = minimal support tickets
- Intercom/Crisp with AI chatbot handles Tier 1
- You handle escalations personally (builds product insight)
- No marketing team needed - PLG + content you write with AI

**Support Math:**
- 10,000 users at 2% monthly contact rate = 200 tickets/month
- AI chatbot deflects 60% = 80 tickets need human review
- 4 tickets/day = easily handled part-time

## Why Ultra-Lean Works for CreatorApp

### 1. Self-Service Product
- No sales team needed (PLG)
- Users onboard themselves
- AI assistant guides users
- Templates reduce support

### 2. AI-Native Operations
- Claude handles 50%+ of coding work
- AI chatbots handle 60-70% of support
- AI writes marketing content
- AI analyzes user data and patterns

### 2.1 AI Platform Costs (Detailed)

AI is a core operational cost that scales with user activity:

| ARR | Users | Annual AI Cost | % of Revenue | Cost/User/Month |
|-----|-------|----------------|--------------|-----------------|
| $1M | 1,100 | $18,000 | 1.8% | $1.36 |
| $2.5M | 2,700 | $43,000 | 1.7% | $1.33 |
| $10M | 10,000 | $165,000 | 1.65% | $1.38 |
| $25M | 27,000 | $400,000 | 1.6% | $1.23 |
| $50M | 54,000 | $800,000 | 1.6% | $1.23 |
| $100M | 108,000 | $1,800,000 | 1.8% | $1.39 |

**Cost Optimization Strategies:**
1. **Task-based model routing**: Use Haiku ($0.80/1M input) for 60% of requests (text generation, colors)
2. **Prompt caching**: 90% reduction on cached system prompts
3. **Batch API**: 50% discount for non-real-time operations

**Model Evolution Planning:**
- Budget 2-4 weeks engineering time per major model migration
- Models deprecated with 6-12 month notice
- Newer models typically offer better price/performance
- Historical trend: 50-70% cost reduction per capability level annually

**Enterprise Note:** Unlimited AI requests for Enterprise tier, but all usage still tracked for platform analytics and capacity planning

### 3. Modern Infrastructure
- Supabase = no DevOps team
- Vercel = no infrastructure team
- Stripe = no billing team
- Resend = no email ops team

### 4. Global Talent Arbitrage
- Nearshore engineers at 40% US cost
- Offshore support at 15% US cost
- Same timezone (LATAM), minimal friction

### 5. No Enterprise Sales
- Self-service up to $997/month
- No sales team, no sales ops
- No customer success managers per account
- Support is reactive, not proactive

## The Critical Inflection Points

### When to Hire (Not Before)

| Trigger | Hire | Not Before |
|---------|------|------------|
| Support queue > 4hr average | Support specialist | This happens |
| You're coding 60hrs/week | First engineer | $500K ARR |
| Bugs piling up, no time | Second engineer | $1M ARR |
| Can't keep up with content | Marketing help | $2M ARR |
| Financial complexity grows | PT Controller | $5M ARR |
| Need strategic ops help | COO | $10M ARR |
| Architecture needs leadership | VP Eng | $15M ARR |
| Product vision needs depth | Head of Product | $20M ARR |

### Functions You Can Delay Forever

1. **HR** - Use Deel, Remote.com, or Oyster. One person can manage 50 people globally.
2. **Legal** - Outside counsel on retainer. No in-house needed until IPO prep.
3. **Dedicated QA** - Engineers + AI + user feedback. Ship fast, fix fast.
4. **Data Team** - AI analytics + founder intuition. No dedicated analysts needed.
5. **Customer Success** - Reactive support + self-service + good product. No proactive CS.
