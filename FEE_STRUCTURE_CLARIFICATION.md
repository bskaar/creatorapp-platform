# CreatorApp Fee Structure

## Overview

CreatorApp operates on a transparent, creator-friendly pricing model. We don't take a cut of your success.

---

## What You Pay

### 1. Platform Subscription (to CreatorApp)

You pay a monthly subscription to use the CreatorApp platform:

- **Starter Plan**: $19/month (14-day free trial)
- **Pro Plan**: $49/month (14-day free trial)
- **Enterprise Plan**: $99/month (14-day free trial)

**This is your only cost to CreatorApp.**

### 2. Stripe Transaction Fees (to Stripe, not CreatorApp)

When you sell products to your customers, Stripe (the payment processor) charges standard fees:

- **US**: 2.9% + $0.30 per successful transaction
- **International**: Varies by country (typically 2.9% + $0.30 or similar)
- **Currency conversion**: Additional 1% if applicable

**These fees go directly to Stripe, not CreatorApp.**

---

## What You DON'T Pay

### ‚ùå No Platform Fees

- **No transaction fees to CreatorApp**: We don't take a percentage of your sales
- **No revenue sharing**: You keep 100% of your revenue (minus Stripe's fees)
- **No additional processing fees**: Only standard Stripe fees apply
- **No hidden charges**: What you see is what you pay

### ‚ùå No Usage Limits

- **Unlimited courses**: Create as many as you want
- **Unlimited students**: No caps on enrollments
- **Unlimited pages**: Build as much content as needed
- **Unlimited email sends**: Based on your plan limits

## Why This Model?

### 1. Predictable Costs

Your monthly subscription is fixed. You know exactly what you'll pay to CreatorApp regardless of your sales volume.

### 2. Scales with Your Success

More sales don't mean higher platform fees. As your revenue grows, your costs stay the same.

### 3. Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees, no surprises. Just your monthly subscription plus standard Stripe fees.

### 4. Stripe Direct

You connect your own Stripe account, so payments go directly to you. CreatorApp never touches your money.

---

## How Stripe Connect Works

1. **You Connect Your Stripe Account**
   - Link your own Stripe account to CreatorApp
   - Payments go directly to your Stripe account
   - You maintain full control

2. **Customer Makes Purchase**
   - Customer buys your course for $100
   - Stripe processes the payment
   - Stripe deducts their fee: $3.20

3. **You Receive Payout**
   - $96.80 is deposited to your bank account
   - According to your Stripe payout schedule
   - No platform fee deducted by CreatorApp

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Does CreatorApp take a percentage of my sales?

**A:** No. CreatorApp charges a flat monthly subscription fee only. We don't take any percentage of your sales or revenue.

### Q: Why do I see Stripe fees?

**A:** Stripe is the payment processor that handles credit card transactions. Their fees (2.9% + $0.30) are industry standard and go directly to Stripe, not CreatorApp.

### Q: Can I avoid Stripe fees?

**A:** No. Credit card processing always incurs fees. Stripe's rates are competitive with other processors like PayPal, Square, etc.

### Q: Do fees change if I sell more?

**A:** Your CreatorApp subscription stays the same regardless of sales volume. Stripe fees remain at 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction. Some high-volume sellers may negotiate custom rates directly with Stripe.

### Q: What if I want to use a different payment processor?

**A:** Currently, CreatorApp exclusively uses Stripe for secure, reliable payment processing. Stripe is integrated deeply into our platform for the best creator experience.

### Q: Are there any other hidden costs?

**A:** No. Your only costs are:
- CreatorApp monthly subscription
- Stripe transaction fees on sales
That's it.
